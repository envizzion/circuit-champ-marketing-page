Circuit Champ Support

Publisher: Envizzion

Need help with Circuit Champ? Email Envizzion at envizzioninc@gmail.com.

Please include:

  • Your device model and Android version
  • The Circuit Champ app version
  • A description of what happened and the steps to reproduce it
  • A screenshot or screen recording, if possible
  • Whether the issue concerns sign-in, cloud saves, an ad, or Circuit Champ Pro purchase/restore

Circuit Champ Pro purchases

If Google Play reports that you already own Circuit Champ Pro, open the app and use Restore purchases while signed in to the same player account. If Pro does not unlock, contact support with the Google Play order number. Do not send full payment-card details or account passwords.

Privacy requests

For privacy or account-linked data deletion requests, email envizzioninc@gmail.com with “Privacy request” in the subject line. See the Privacy Policy for details.
